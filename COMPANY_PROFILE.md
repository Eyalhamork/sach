## 1. Executive Summary

**SACH Trucking and Logistics LLC** is a premier, dynamic service provider based in **Monrovia, Liberia**, specializing in two core sectors: **Logistics & Freight Transportation** and **Car Rental Services**.

Founded in **2025**, SACH was established to address the critical need for reliable, professional, and safe transportation solutions in Liberia and the wider West African region. We are a "one-stop" transportation partner, offering seamless supply chain solutions for businesses and premium mobility options for individuals and organizations.

Whether moving heavy cargo across borders or providing comfortable, rugged 4x4 vehicles for corporate travel, SACH is dedicated to operational excellence, safety, and customer satisfaction.

## 3. Our Services

SACH operates through two primary service divisions:

### A. Logistics & Supply Chain Solutions
We provide comprehensive freight and logistics services designed to keep supply chains moving efficiently.

*   **Trucking & Freight Transportation:**
    *   Reliable **long-distance and local hauling** for goods of all sizes.
    *   Fleet capable of handling containerized cargo, bulk goods, and construction materials.
*   **Supply Chain Solutions:**
    *   End-to-end logistics planning and route optimization to reduce costs and transit times.
*   **Warehousing & Distribution:**
    *   Secure storage facilities and efficient distribution support for inventory management.
*   **Customs Clearance Support:**
    *   Assistance with documentation and regulatory compliance to ensure smooth cross-border movement.
*   **Specialized Transport:**
    *   Handling of project cargo, oversized loads, and time-critical shipments.

### B. Car Rental Services
Our Car Rental division offers premium mobility solutions tailored for comfort, safety, and the rugged demands of Liberian roads.

*   **Corporate Fleet Leasing:**
    *   Long-term rental solutions for **NGOs, Government Agencies, and Corporate businesses**.
    *   Fully maintained fleets to ensure zero downtime for your operations.
*   **Short-Term & Daily Rentals:**
    *   Flexible rental options for business trips, vacations, or temporary needs.
*   **Chauffeur & Driver Services:**
    *   Professional, vetted, and experienced drivers who know the local terrain, ensuring a safe and stress-free journey.
*   **Airport Transfers:**
    *   Reliable pick-up and drop-off services at **Roberts International Airport (RIA)** and other hubs.
*   **Event Transportation:**
    *   Coordination of transport for delegations, conferences, and weddings.

**Vehicle Categories:**
*   **Standard SUVs:** (e.g., Toyota RAV4, Hyundai Tucson) for city and light off-road use.
*   **Premium 4x4s:** (e.g., Toyota Land Cruiser, Prado) for heavy-duty upcountry travel and VIP comfort.
*   **Sedans:** Economical options for city commuting.
*   **Buses & Vans:** For staff transport and group travel.
